Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AutoAttend

How does the facial recognition attendance work?
Our system uses advanced facial recognition algorithms to identify students as they enter the classroom. It captures facial features and matches them with pre-registered student profiles, automatically marking attendance in real-time.
What happens if there's no internet connection?
AutoAttend works offline! The system stores attendance data locally and automatically syncs with our servers once an internet connection is restored. This makes it perfect for rural areas with limited connectivity.
