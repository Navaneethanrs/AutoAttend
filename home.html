The Problem We Solve

Manual Attendance Systems Are Inefficient

Many rural schools still depend on manual attendance systems which are time-consuming and inaccurate. Teachers spend valuable instructional time on attendance-taking, and record-keeping is prone to errors.

Our solution automates attendance using facial recognition or RFID to save teachers' time and improve accuracy, allowing them to focus on what matters most - teaching.

Key Features

Facial Recognition / RFID

Automated attendance marking using advanced facial recognition or RFID technology.

Real-time Tracking

Monitor attendance in real-time with instant updates and notifications.

Mobile & Offline Mode

Works seamlessly on mobile devices even in low-internet areas.

Attendance Reports

Generate daily, weekly, and monthly attendance reports with analytics.

Absentee Alerts

Automatic alerts for absent students sent to teachers and parents.

Low-cost Solution

Affordable technology designed specifically for rural school budgets.
