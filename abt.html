About Us

Transforming Education Through Technology

AutoAttend was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful mission: to revolutionize attendance tracking in rural schools through innovative, affordable technology solutions.

Our journey began when our founders witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by teachers in rural areas who spent valuable instructional time on manual attendance processes. We realized that technology could provide a simple, effective solution to this widespread problem.

Today, AutoAttend serves over 200 schools across rural India, helping teachers save thousands of hours annually while improving the accuracy of attendance records and enabling better educational outcomes.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower rural schools with affordable, easy-to-use technology that automates attendance tracking, saves teachers' time, and improves educational outcomes for students.

Our Vision

To create a future where every rural school in India has access to smart attendance systems, enabling teachers to focus on what matters most - quality education.

Our Promise

We commit to providing reliable, cost-effective solutions tailored specifically for the unique challenges faced by rural educational institutions.
